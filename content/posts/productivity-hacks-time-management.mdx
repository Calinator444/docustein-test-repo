## Setting Your Goals

Begin by defining one to three clear, measurable goals (for example: reduce weekly meetings by 25%, increase focused deep-work time by 2 hours/week, or complete a project milestone by a specific date). Break each goal into concrete weekly actions and a simple metric you can review every week.

## Creating Effective Routines

Design routines that support your goals: batch similar tasks, schedule uninterrupted deep-work blocks, and protect energy by planning short breaks and realistic daily workloads.

### Morning Rituals

A useful morning routine can include 10 minutes of planning, a short movement or breathing exercise, and identifying the top three priorities for the day — habits that prime focus and momentum.

### Evening Wind-Down

Use a 20–30 minute evening routine to review progress, note wins and blockers, prepare tomorrow’s top priorities, and disconnect from screens to improve sleep quality.
