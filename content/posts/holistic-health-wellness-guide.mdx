## The Importance of Mental Wellness

Mental wellness is foundational to overall health: include daily practices like mindfulness, brief reflection, and regular social connection to support resilience and emotional balance. Prioritize small, consistent habits over dramatic one-time changes.

## Building Healthy Habits

Focus on habit stacking and environmental design: pair a new habit with an existing routine, make the desired behavior easy to start, and track progress with simple metrics to build momentum.

### Nutrition Fundamentals

Aim for balanced meals with proteins, healthy fats, whole grains, and plenty of vegetables; plan simple, repeatable meals and limit highly processed foods to maintain energy and cognitive clarity.

### Exercise and Movement

Incorporate a mix of aerobic activity, strength training, and daily movement breaks. Start with realistic targets (e.g., 150 minutes moderate activity per week) and choose activities you enjoy for long-term adherence.
