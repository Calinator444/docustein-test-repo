## The Startup Landscape

Startups succeed when they align a clear problem with a viable market and a repeatable business model; begin by defining your target customer, their pain points, and the core value your product provides. Perform a lightweight competitor scan to understand existing solutions and identify gaps your product can address.

## Validating Your Idea

Validate hypotheses early and cheaply: build a one-page value proposition, create simple landing pages or explainer videos, and measure interest through sign-ups or pre-orders rather than building a full product first.

### Market Research

Use a mix of quantitative data (market size, search trends) and qualitative interviews to refine your target segment; prioritize channels where early customers are already congregating.

### Customer Discovery

Run 5–10 problem interviews to surface real user pain, prototype minimal experiments (surveys, ad tests, concierge MVPs), and track conversion or engagement metrics to decide whether to iterate or pivot.
